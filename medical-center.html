Why We Serve Free

At Al-Zahra Medical Centre, we believe that quality healthcare is a fundamental right, not a privilege. Our mission is to ensure that every member of our community has access to professional medical care, regardless of their financial situation. Through the generous support of our donors and dedicated medical professionals, we provide comprehensive healthcare services completely free of charge.
